FEATURES

Fun and Interactive Games

Kidly provides a fun and interactive way of learning in the form of games like Memory Game, Jeopardy , Wordle and Word Association. These not encourage children to play mind developing games but can also help them gain General Knowlegde, basic understanding of maths, Science and improve their English Vocabulary.Playing these games daily can have quite a positive effect on their brain and can tilt them towards studies at such an early age and at the same time hlps the parents who struggle to spare time to help children with their mind development at initial stages of life. .

Healthy Competitions and Quizzes

The facility of quizzes on Kidly is a wonderful geature to inculcate sportsmanspirit in children, to make them understand the importance of adoring both the situations, even if they win or lose. It also teaches them the importance of time and builds a focus which a must if you are facing an opponent in a quiz or a healthy game competition. The idea of winning and learning something new can keep them hooked up to the quizzes which will eventually improve their general knowledge and hold over a subject.

Daily Progress Check and Leaderboards

The most useful of all the features is the concept of leaderboard which not only tracks your child's position on the basis of games and quizzes attempted but also tracks daily progress of your child. The leaderboard is dynamic and gives equal weightage to the amount of knowldege gained in a day and to the number of quizzes one and has participated in and won .There is a different leaderboard for each age group since the games and the study material is also divides according to the level of mind development. This is supposed to make the children visit Kidly daily and spend loads of time playing and studying.
